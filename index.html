FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Iraq Ziyarat package?

Our Iraq Ziyarat packages include return flights from major Indian cities, Iraq visa processing, hotel accommodation in Baghdad, Karbala, and Najaf, daily meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner), AC transport for all transfers and Ziyarat, a knowledgeable religious guide, travel insurance, and 24/7 on-ground support.

Which sacred sites are covered in Karbala?

In Karbala, you will visit the Shrine of Imam Hussain (AS), Shrine of Hazrat Abbas (AS), Tillul Zainabiya, and surrounding sacred sites. Daily prayers are conducted at the shrine complex.

Do you offer Iran Ziyarat packages?

Yes, we offer dedicated Iran Ziyarat packages covering Mashhad (Shrine of Imam Reza AS), Qom (Shrine of Hazrat Masuma SA), Isfahan, and Shiraz. We also offer combined Iraq + Iran packages.

What currency should I carry for Iraq?

Iraq primarily uses the US Dollar for tourist transactions. We advise pilgrims to carry sufficient USD for personal expenses like shopping, tips, and any extras outside the package inclusions.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 to 3 months in advance. Arbaeen and Gyarvi Sharif packages sell out quickly due to limited seats, so earlier booking is always better.

What is the payment process?

We require a 25% advance at the time of booking to confirm your seat. The remaining balance is due 30 days before departure. We accept bank transfer, UPI, credit/debit cards, and EMI options.
